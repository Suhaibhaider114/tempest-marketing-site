The Process

End-to-End. Zero Gaps.

Every stage of the pipeline is engineered for speed, precision, and complete accountability — from the moment a prospect submits a form to the moment they appear on your calendar.

01
Paid Traffic

Targeted Inbound Traffic

Whether you are running Meta Ads, Google PPC, or working with purchased lead lists, your traffic flows into your landing pages or CRM. The moment a prospect submits a contact form, the Tempest Marketing system is triggered automatically — with zero manual intervention required.

✓ Meta Ads & Google PPC ✓ Landing page form capture ✓ CRM webhook triggers ✓ Third-party lead list import
02
Speed-to-Lead

Instant AI Outreach

Within 3 to 5 minutes of a form submission, our AI initiates direct contact with the prospect. It does not sleep, take breaks, or observe weekends. By engaging the prospect at peak buying intent — immediately after they have raised their hand — we capture attention before any competitor has the chance to respond.

✓ Under 5-minute response ✓ AI voice + SMS + email ✓ 24/7 — no exceptions ✓ Peak buying-intent engagement
03
Pre-Screening

Dynamic AI Qualification

The AI conducts a natural, conversational qualification dialogue. It verifies the exact criteria you define during onboarding — budget range, timeline, motivation level, and specific fit requirements. Prospects who do not meet your minimum criteria are politely disqualified before they ever touch your calendar.

✓ Budget verification ✓ Timeline confirmation ✓ Problem fit validation ✓ Custom disqualification rules
04
Calendar Sync

Direct Calendar Booking

Once a prospect passes qualification, the AI books them directly into your available calendar slots — synced in real time with your CRM and calendar platform. Confirmation messages and reminders are sent automatically. No back-and-forth. No phone tag. No leads stalling in a follow-up queue.

✓ Real-time calendar sync ✓ Automated confirmations & reminders ✓ Full qualification notes attached ✓ Google Calendar & Calendly native
05
You Close

The Human Handoff

Your team opens their calendar to see fully briefed, pre-qualified prospects. Every booking includes the full transcript of the AI's qualification conversation, confirmed budget and timeline, and recommended talking points. Your closers walk into every call knowing exactly who they are speaking with — and ready to close, not qualify.

✓ Full AI conversation transcript ✓ Qualification summary report ✓ Recommended talking points ✓ CRM record auto-populated
