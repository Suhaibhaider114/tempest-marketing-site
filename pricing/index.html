No Ambiguity.

Every question a serious buyer asks before making a decision — answered directly and completely.

You should expect 20 pre-qualified, booked appointments within 60 days of your system going live. That is not an estimate or an average — it is our contractual commitment. The system is typically live within 2–3 weeks of onboarding. From go-live, the 60-day clock starts. Every appointment is pre-screened against your exact qualification criteria: budget, timeline, and fit. You are not receiving leads — you are receiving confirmed calendar meetings with qualified prospects.
Tempest Marketing works exclusively with high-ticket businesses where a single qualified appointment directly translates to meaningful revenue. Our three core verticals are real estate professionals (agents, brokerages, investors), marketing and creative agencies selling monthly retainers, and B2B consultants or service firms with ticket sizes of $3,000 or more per engagement. This system is not designed for e-commerce, low-ticket consumer products, or businesses without an active or planned inbound lead source. If you are not currently running ads or do not have an inbound channel, we can build that as part of the Advanced Package.
From the date you sign and complete onboarding, our team typically requires 2 to 3 weeks to build, test, and deploy the full AI system. This includes CRM integration, qualification scripting, calendar sync, and go-live testing. We manage the entire technical buildout. The time commitment on your end is minimal — primarily a 1-hour onboarding call to define your qualification criteria and review your existing CRM and calendar setup.
The Setup Package ($2,500) includes the complete core AI appointment system: speed-to-lead activation, qualification logic, CRM and calendar integration, and the 60-day guarantee. It is the right choice for businesses with an active inbound lead source who need to close the follow-up gap immediately. The Advanced / Growth Package ($5,000) adds multi-channel outreach (voice, SMS, and email), a full ad campaign build, advanced A/B tested qualification sequences, a pipeline analytics dashboard, and 90 days of post-launch optimization support. It is designed for businesses ready to scale their pipeline comprehensively. Both packages carry the same 20-appointment, 60-day guarantee.
You own the system completely after deployment. Tempest Marketing does not charge ongoing retainers for the AI infrastructure. Once built, tested, and handed over, it is your business asset — the scripts, the workflows, the integrations. Your only ongoing costs are the underlying platform subscriptions you use (such as your CRM, Twilio for telephony, or your ad platforms), which typically run $50–$150/month depending on volume. Optional performance optimization support is available separately for clients who want ongoing refinement, but it is never required to maintain the system's operation.
To guarantee 20 qualified appointments within 60 days, we recommend an active paid traffic budget of $2,000–$5,000 per month directed at your target market. This ad spend goes directly to the advertising platforms (Meta, Google, etc.) — it is not included in our setup fee. If you are not currently running paid campaigns, the Advanced / Growth Package includes full ad campaign architecture and launch as part of the buildout. For the Setup Package, we can advise on campaign structure during onboarding, though the media spend management is handled by your team.
