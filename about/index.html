About Tempest Marketing

We Build Systems.
You Close Deals.

Tempest Marketing is a U.S.-based growth infrastructure company. We specialize in one thing: building AI-powered appointment systems that deliver qualified, booked meetings for high-ticket businesses — backed by a result-or-refund guarantee.

Our Mission

Infrastructure That
Delivers, Not Promises.

Most marketing agencies sell you deliverables — ad campaigns, funnels, content, and reports. We sell you one thing: qualified appointments on your calendar, with a contractual guarantee behind every deployment.

We built Tempest Marketing because we kept seeing the same problem in high-ticket businesses across real estate, consulting, and agency verticals. The lead generation was working. The offers were strong. The closers were capable. But the pipeline was broken — not because of the product, but because of the gap between a prospect submitting a form and a human picking up the phone.

That gap — in most businesses — is measured in hours. In our system, it is measured in minutes. That difference alone changes the entire trajectory of a business's growth.

"The average business takes 47 hours to follow up with an inbound lead. The first business to respond wins 78% of all deals. We close that gap — permanently."

What We Do

One System. Guaranteed Results.

We do not manage ad accounts. We do not write blog content. We do not run social media. We build one thing — a fully automated appointment infrastructure — and we guarantee it performs.

01

AI-Powered Speed-to-Lead

We deploy AI systems that respond to every inbound lead within 3–5 minutes, 24 hours a day. The technology eliminates the single biggest leak in most high-ticket pipelines: slow follow-up.

02

Automated Qualification

Our AI engages prospects in natural, conversational dialogue — verifying budget, timeline, and fit before any human interaction occurs. Your team only speaks with people who are genuinely ready.

03

Direct Calendar Booking

Qualified prospects are booked directly onto your calendar with full context notes attached. No scheduling friction. No back-and-forth. No leads falling through cracks.

04

Full System Ownership Transfer

Once deployed and live, the entire AI infrastructure becomes your business asset. We do not lock you into retainers. You own the system, the scripts, and the workflow outright.

05

The 60-Day Guarantee

Every deployment is backed by our unambiguous performance guarantee: 20 qualified appointments in 60 days, or a full refund of your setup fee. No conditions. No exceptions. We carry the risk.
